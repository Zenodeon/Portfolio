Omin

Omin is a semi-realistic first-person cinematic short, created in Unreal Engine as part of a university project. It explores a chilling encounter with an ancient, mythic beingâ€”what begins as legend quickly unravels into a haunting sequence of events.

I handled the creative direction, from narrative pacing to visual composition and lighting. To streamline production, I also developed a custom movement tool inside Unreal Engine to simplify character motion blocking in first-person scenes, allowing for faster iteration and more expressive in-engine storytelling.

The final piece blends real-time rendering, atmosphere, and sound design into a focused cinematic experience.

Tools & Software:
- Unreal Engine
- Blender
- Adobe After Effects
- Adobe Audition

Audio & SFX:
